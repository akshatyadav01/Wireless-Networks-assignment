About IET DAVV

Institute of Engineering & Technology (IET) is one of the premier engineering institutes of Madhya Pradesh, established in 1996 as an autonomous institute under Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore. The institute has evolved into a center of excellence in technical education.

IET DAVV offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering and technology. The institute has well-equipped laboratories, workshops, and a rich central library to support academic and research activities.

The institute maintains a close relationship with industry to provide students with practical exposure and enhance their employability. IET DAVV has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in Central India.
